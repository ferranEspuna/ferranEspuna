# Ferran Espuña: PhD Student in Mathematics, Research Engineer, and Mathematician

I am a first-year PhD student in mathematics working in arithmetic combinatorics, with a focus on higher-order Fourier analysis and its connections to harmonic analysis and ergodic theory. I hold a double Bachelor's degree in mathematics and computer science, and a Master's degree in Advanced Mathematics.
